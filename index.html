Our Story

MMJ blossomed from a magical moment when Kushal held up a sign at the airport, professing his love for his wife, Mariam. The image of their heartfelt reunion touched countless hearts and went viral. Inspired by this enchanting experience, they created a brand that captures the essence of love, romance, and cherished memories, turning their dream into a reality.
